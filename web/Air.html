Aerial Perspective, 2023

In painting, to create a sense of depth and give space to a flat surface, artists use Aerial (from Greek aerios "of the air, pertaining to air,") perspective, where objects become progressively blurrier as they recede into the background.
Applying this principle, the website operates by detecting the air exhaled from the user's lips. Through this, it encourages reflection on how spatial perception is constructed on the plane of a computer display.

회화에서 깊이감을 주고 평평한 표면에 공간감을 만들기 위해 대기 원근법(Aerial perspective)을 사용합니다. 이는 물체가 배경으로 멀어질수록 점점 더 흐릿해지는 방식으로, '공기와 관련된'이라는 의미를 가진 그리스어 'aerios'에서 유래되었습니다.
